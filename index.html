Problem Statement

In this era of the digital age, information has become more accessible to us than ever before. We live in a world where there is an endless supply of content, generated to meet the
ever growing need for it.

However, the longer we spend on our screen the more we blur the boundaries between what is excessive and what is common place. We are growing accustomed to information
overloads; that inevitably take a toll on our mental health. This is especially prevalent in the case of information that could trigger a user, such as world news, fringe content, etc.

Therefore, a pressing need of the hour is to provide users with a means of consuming their information in a regulated and mindful manner.



MoodEye:Your Digital Guardian

Our application MoodEye is built with the sole purpose of regulating the digital consumption of users, by keeping them mindful of their emotional state while browsing on the Internet.

Once activated, it will keep track of a user's facial expressions for the duration of their browsing session. A predefined threshold has been set for each emotion. Once that threshold has been crossed, an overlay window will cover the screen for a period of 10 minutes. This serves as a digital time-out for the user.

This forces the user to temporarily disconnect and separate themselves from the content they were consuming at the time, thereby reducing the likelihood of them falling into an information "rabbit-hole".
